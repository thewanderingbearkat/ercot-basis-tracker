Basis = node LMP − hub LMP, decomposed into the binding constraints that drive it. Each line's contribution = its effect on the node minus its effect on the hub. Left = pushing basis down (toward a cheaper node), right = pushing it up. Down + Up = the congestion; the small residual (5-min/hourly timing + reference) closes to the total basis in the box. ERCOT shift factors are authoritative (actual 5-min RT), so the per-constraint split is exact — no model noise (unlike the modeled PJM split). Click a row to locate it on the map.
